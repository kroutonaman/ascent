Import/Export Services and Customs Brokerage

ASCENT offer’s customized Import/Export logistics solutions to meet your global transportation and customs brokerage requirements. Our team process your entries in a timely manner at a competitive cost. Our customers have come to rely on Ascent’s successful history of Customs brokerage services.

Customs services include:

  •   Customs clearance at all ports and airports
  •   24/7 customs entries processing and clearance
  •   Online duty payment
  •   Formal and informal entries
  •   Warehouse entries
  •   Temporary Import Bond
  •   Duty drawback procedures
  •   Coordinating with other government agencies
